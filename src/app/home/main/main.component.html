About Us

Scour Laundry and Dry Clean provides you with the laundry services and laundromat facilities you need to get you laundry done cleaner. With ozone sanitized washers, you can be sure you're always getting a clean machine that sanitizes your clothing and linens with ozone water every cycle.

Enjoy the peace of mind that a sanitized washer gives you, whether you do it yourself or have us do your laundry for you. With available dry cleaning service, we're you're one stop shop for all your clothing and linen cleaning needs!
